Al-Ballor Industry Company is one of the promising companies in the industrial business sector, which was established in the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia in 2022 AD, where Al-Balour Industry Company manages the Barrak Industrial Gases Factory and the Barrak Glass Factory

Where the work of the Barrak Glass Factory began in 2005 AD in the treatment of types of glass formations such as tempering, and then the development took place to add a new production line such as the production of curved glass and others. In 2008, the Barak Industrial Gases Factory was established, as it produces and provides all types of industrial, medical and food gases at the highest levels of quality.

Vision: Enhancing competitiveness, adopting the best administrative systems and practices, and providing distinguished service to our customers and partners in success in various regions of the Kingdom

The message: That the crystal company is always the preferred choice for our customers, by reaching the quality of the product to the highest international quality standards
